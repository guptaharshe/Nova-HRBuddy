# **NovaTech Pvt Ltd — Employee Handbook**

## **1\. Leave Policy**

* **Paid Leave (PL):** Every full-time employee is entitled to **24 paid leaves per calendar year**, credited at 2 per month.  
* **Unpaid Leave:** Employees may additionally take up to **30 unpaid leaves per calendar year**, subject to manager approval.  
* **Carry-forward:** Up to 10 unused paid leaves may be carried forward to the next calendar year. Anything beyond that lapses.  
* **Sick Leave:** Included within the 24 paid leaves; no separate sick leave bucket.  
* **Leave Approval:** All leave (paid or unpaid) must be approved by the employee's reporting manager at least 2 working days in advance, except medical emergencies.  
* The chatbot cannot approve or reject leave requests — employees must apply through their manager or the HR portal.

## **2\. Work From Home (WFH) / Hybrid Policy**

* Standard work mode is **hybrid**: minimum 3 days/week in office, remaining days flexible.  
* Employees may request full WFH for up to 5 consecutive working days per quarter with manager approval.  
* Extended WFH beyond this requires HR approval on a case-by-case basis.

## **3\. Expense Reimbursement**

* Reimbursable categories: travel for official work, client meeting expenses, internet reimbursement (up to ₹1,000/month for remote roles), approved training/certification costs.  
* Claims must be submitted within 30 days of expense, with valid bills, through the expense portal.  
* Reimbursements are processed within 15 working days of approval.

## **4\. Notice Period & Probation**

* Standard probation period: **3 months** from date of joining.  
* Notice period during probation: **15 days**.  
* Notice period after confirmation: **60 days** for all roles below manager level, **90 days** for manager and above.

## **5\. Public Holidays**

NovaTech observes 10 public holidays per year, published annually on the HR portal in January. Regional holidays may vary by office location.

## **6\. General Conduct**

* All employees are expected to maintain professional conduct and confidentiality regarding client and company data.  
* Any grievances regarding harassment, discrimination, or workplace conduct should be raised directly with HR through the confidential grievance channel — not through this chatbot.

---

*This handbook covers general company-wide policy only. For personalized details (leave balance, salary, joining date), employees should ask the chatbot after logging in, or contact HR directly for anything not covered here.*
